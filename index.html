DnDice

I made DnDice, when running a game of Dungeons & Dragons over Christmas, everyone was playing for the first time and no one had dice. After seeing the visually busy options on the app store, I decided something much simpler would be really helpful, just to get everyone up and running.

My aim was to make it as clean, simple and uncomplicated as possible. Press one of the buttons and a random dice roll will be made for the appropriate dice.
